where Mountains Meet

where Mountains Meet was founded in 2015 by Genevieve Saylak and Corissa Santos. The collection demonstrates that impeccable style can go hand in hand with thoughtful living. That responsibly sourced materials and industry best practices are fundamental building blocks of superior design.
